Frequently asked questions

  • Why is this FAQ so short?

    Not many questions have been asked yet. Ask one!

  • Who can use this site?

    This web site is accessible to all current MIT students via Touchstone authentication. For the fall 2020 semester only classes listed in course 18 are available on pset partners. Depending on how it goes this fall, we may expand in the spring.

  • Why are all the dorm and living group locations grayed-out in the location drop down?

    Given that only a small proportion of the student population will be on campus this fall we didn't think it made sense to optimize pset groups according to physical location. The "in-person" option for communication is disabled for the same reason (that doesn't mean you cannot meet anyone in person, simply that you can't set a preference for this on the pset partners website).
